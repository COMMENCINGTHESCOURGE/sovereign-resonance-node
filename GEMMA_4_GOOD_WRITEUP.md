# PROJECT: THE SOVEREIGN RESONANCE NODE
## Track: Digital Equity & Inclusivity | Special Track: Ollama / llama.cpp
**Team:** Guinea Pig Trench LLC

### 1. THE VISION: PREVENTING SYSTEMIC INJUSTICE
In 2015, a mathematical error in the Washington State Department of Corrections database resulted in 3,200 prisoners being held past their legal release dates. This wasn't a failure of law; it was a failure of **Verification**. The Sovereign Resonance Node is an agentic cognitive prosthetic designed to ensure that no individual is ever "lost in the code."

### 2. ARCHITECTURE: THE SCARBOROUGH ROPE ENGINE
Our system utilizes **Gemma 4 2B** as a Gated Recalibrator. By monitoring "Logical Torsion"—the density and friction of legal/arithmetic text—the node triggers frontier-level reasoning only when a potential "Arithmetic Illegality" is detected. This preserves local VRAM while maintaining state-of-the-art accuracy on edge hardware.

### 3. TECHNICAL DEPTH: BEYOND PIXELS
We demonstrate the node's power through the **ARC (Abstraction & Reasoning Corpus)** challenge. By porting winning C++ primitives to local Python and using Gemma 4 to sequence them, we move from "probabilistic guessing" to "deterministic verification."

### 4. VISUAL RESONANCE: THE TRENCH STANDARD
Every logic transition is verified via the **TRENCH·FLIPBOOK**, a CRT-atmosphere auditing suite that allows humans to rapidly-scroll through AI reasoning steps, ensuring transparency and trust.
